Chaya

Branding Design

OVERVIEW

Japanese
vegetarian restaurant

Chaya is a Japanese-style vegetarian restaurant in San Francisco with a good service and delicious dishes. As an old restaurant that has been in business for more than a decade, Chaya has never had its own brand design. This program attempts to inspire the new vitality of this affluent restaurant through the design of its brand and website.

BACKGOUND

Japanese buddhist cuisine

Cha-Ya Vegetarian Japanese Cuisine is a family owned and operated restaurant in San Francisco’s Mission District. Cha-Ya serves contemporary Japanese vegetarian and all vegan cusine inspired by "Shojin Ryori," or Japanese Buddhist cuisine.


Specialties include stuffed eggplant tempura, vegan sukiyaki, various traditional soba and udon noodle soups, as well as all vegan sushi. The Cha-Ya Roll is our signature sushi roll; a tempura sushi topped with a soy ginger glaze. All of Cha-ya’s dishes are vegan, and inspired by Zen cooking.
