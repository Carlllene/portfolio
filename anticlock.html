OVERVIEW

Refresh the shopping experience
for the elders

Even if you always do your best to eat a well-rounded diet filled with lots of fruits and vegetables, you likely still have some gaps in your nutrition. Certain vitamins and minerals, like vitamin D and vitamin B2, can be very difficult to get from food alone. This is where taking supplements can come in handy.


With Anticlock, you’ll get a personalized daily vitamin pack made with better ingredients along with honest guidance to help you find the perfect supplements.

BACKGOUND

More than half of
Americans take vitamins

Vitamin-obsessed people are a group of people popping vitamin supplements. They believe supplements can prevent diseases and improve people’s health.


More than half of Americans take vitamins or other dietary supplements, according to The Council for Responsible Nutrition, and in 2015 alone, they spent $21 billion on them, according to HealthLine.

PROBLEMS

No care

Data from studies of acute hospitalization in older patients suggest that up to 71 percent are at nutritional risk or are malnourished. Increaseing with age, the elders‘ diet and lifestyle are changed, so their vitamins should be too. There is high demand for the elders to buy the intamins independently.

Limited Service

Online Experience

Lack of Trust

SOLUTIONS

Anticlock

Based on every senior’s diet, lifestyle and health considerations, we provides personalized supplement with a combination of vitamins by friendly interface. We build a complete and smooth online shopping experience for the elders, but the operiation is easier. We will connect to users’ doctor or nutrionitst to giving more scientific and more reliable tailored guidance.
