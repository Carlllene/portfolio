PROBLEMS

Lack of visitors’ communication

1

There has to be an interaction between the observer and curator through a specific object or exhibit. But most are between visitors and exhibit.

2

Good exhibitions combine learning and the ability to communicate its results to the majority of its visitors. Fun Interaction.

3

There are a lot of thinking and feelings during exploring the exhibit.

IDEATION

Final Solution

After 5 visitors voted, choose the 2nd solution.


To project the visitors’ comments or feeling after viewing the exhibit on floor closed to the exhibit, finally share with others exploring the same exhibition or on the internet. This projection can also be instead of the physical barrier of the exhibit. Then this data will upload to the internet to help people know more about this exhibition.

STORY BOARD

New experience

1. Cici is a designer, and she likes exploring different kinds of exhibitions when she is free.

2. She usually checks the exhibition which she will be interested in online, then checks the exhibition information and buys the ticket.

3. When she is viewing the exhibit, she always has some feelings for the exhibit to share. But it’s so noisy to whisper that may disturb others.

4. She finds that there are several icons showing different feelings on the floor.

5. She steps on the sad icon same as her feeling the exhibit in the front of her gives to her.

6. Then a sad animation is played on the floor between the boundary for the exhibit and the wall.

7. Finally, the system record the Cici’s feeling and share with other visitors interested in this exhibit. The people preparing to come to this exhibition get more feedbacks from this record.
