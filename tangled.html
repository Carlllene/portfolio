BRAND STORY

Tangled

There were a lot of kids in need. The various causes of hair loss in children don’t result in just physical suffering. The change in their appearance can drastically undermine their self-image and sabotage their self-esteem.


To help heal the pain of these struggles, we collect monetary and hair donations used to create wigs made from real hair. These custom hair replacement systems are supplied to families at no cost to them. We depend on donations and volunteers to support our mission.


We want to give these kids the opportunity to feel good about themselves again. If you’re interested in donating hair for kids, choosing an organization can be challenging. There are many great causes out there. Tangled is a nonprofit that helps children suffering from hair loss by creating wigs made of natural hair.


Our goal is to give each child the ability to look themselves and live their life with a smile and confidence.
